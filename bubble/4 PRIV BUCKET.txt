
1. Log in to your AWS account (as root user).
2. Search for **Amazon S3** in the search bar.
3. Click **Create Bucket**.
4. Enter a **unique bucket name** (no spaces, no capital letters).
5. Choose region: **Asia Pacific (Mumbai) – ap-south-1**.
6. Keep all default settings (no changes needed).
7. Click **Create Bucket**.
8. Click on your bucket name.
9. Click **Upload**.
10. Click **Add Files** and choose a file from your computer.
11. Click **Upload** to upload the file.
12. After upload, you will see the file in the bucket.
13. Select the file.
14. Click **Actions → Share with presigned URL**.
15. Set time limit and click **Create URL**.
16. Paste the link in browser → file will open.
